Lottery

Running the lottery will randomly approve one shuttle per time slot for which multiple competing shuttles have been requested. If only one shuttle for a particular time slot has been requested, that shuttle will automatically be approved. Shuttles that are not chosen in the lottery will remain in "Pending" status in case an approved shuttle is cancelled before departure. In the event of this, a "Pending" shuttle can be manually approved on the shuttle dashboard to fill the open slot.
