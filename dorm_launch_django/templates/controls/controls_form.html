Lottery

Running the lottery will randomly approve one shuttle per time slot for which multiple shuttles have been requested. If only one shuttle for a time slot has been requested, that shuttle will automatically be approved. Shuttles that are not chosen in the lottery will retain a "Pending" status in case an approved shuttle is cancelled before departure. In the event of this, a "Pending" shuttle can be manually approved on the Dashboard to fill the open slot.
